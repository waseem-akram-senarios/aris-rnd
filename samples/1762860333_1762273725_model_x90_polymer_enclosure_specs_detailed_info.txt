CATEGORY: Design & Features
======================================================================

Q1: What are all design features and technical specifications?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Answer:
The design features and technical specifications of the Model X-90 High-Density Polymer Enclosure include:
- Main body material: Polycarbonate (PC) $\text{UL94-VO}$ rated, UV-stabilized, color: RAL 7035 (Light Grey)
- Gasket material: Silicone Rubber, Shore Hardness A 50 ± 5, must meet $\text{IP65}$ rating standards
- Fasteners: Stainless Steel $\text{ASTM A240, Type 304}$
- Overall dimensions: $150.00 \text{mm} \times 100.00 \text{mm} \times 45.00 \text{mm}$ (Length $\times$ Width $\times$ Height) with tolerances of $\pm 0.30 \text{mm}$
- Wall Thickness: $2.50 \text{mm}$ nominal with a tolerance of $+0.20 \text{mm}/ -0.05 \text{mm}$
- Mounting Holes: $\text{M3}$ threaded inserts required in four corner posts with a position tolerance of $\pm 0.10 \text{mm}$
- Molding process: Injection molding with draft angles $\ge 1.5^\circ$ and limited flash of $\le 0.10 \text{mm}$ at the parting line
- Surface Finish: External surface finish $\text{MT-11010}$ (Medium Texture), interior surface finish $\text{SPI A-3}$
- Post-Molding Requirements: Installation of four brass heat-set inserts ($\text{M3} \times 5 \text{mm}$ length) and ensuring cleanliness of the gasket channel
- Quality Assurance and Inspection: Dimensional verification, leak test for $\text{IP65}$ rating confirmation, visual inspection for warpage and sinks.
